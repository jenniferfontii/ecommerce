FIAMMETTA GIANFANTI
The Artist

Fiammetta was born in Cesena, Italy on the 6th of April 1945
She developed a huge passion for art since her childhood when she had her first contact with the art industries.
Lately, during her teenage years Fiammetta attended the art Academy in Ravenna, Italy.
During those years she lived alone in a college and met her now husband Umberto.
With Umberto she traveled all along sicily where she got many ideas for her paintings.
After marrying him they moved to Turin, Italy where they spent around 5 years of their life, to move back to Ravenna.
Although, most of the paintings in this website are from the late 90's to 2019.
In fact, during those years she managed to get most of her famous paintings done.
